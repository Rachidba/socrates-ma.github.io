SoCraTes-FR is a two-day Open-Space conference. This event is non-commercial and community-driven. It will take place at the beautiful castle "Château de Rochegude" - France (Dôme). The official trailer is availabe now:

Call for Sponsors

SoCraTes-FR is an international non-profit conference for crafts(wo)men by crafts(wo)men. We are looking for sponsors to help make this event affordable for our attendees. If you are interested in supporting us and make SoCraTes happens in France, please send us an email.

How to get to the “Château de Rochegude” ?

The “château de Rochegude” is located 1h20’ from Marseille, 1h45’ from Lyon, 1h15’ from Montpellier and 1h from Aix-En-Provence

By Train

The closest stations are : “Gare d’Orange” - 12 Km “Gare TGV d’Avignon” - 40 Km

By Plane

Avignon Airport - 50 Km Marseille Airport - 120 Km

SoCraTes Family

SoCraTes-FR is a part of a big family. The first “Software Craftsmanship and Testing (un)conference” took place a few years ago in Germany and is organised every year since 2011.

Other conferences have emerged in other European countries such as SoCraTes UK and SoCraTes Canaries.
