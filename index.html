Let's self-organize : Make your input to the wiki

SoCraTes-FR is a two-day Open-Space conference. This event is non-commercial and community-driven. It will take place at the beautiful castle "Château de Rochegude" - France (Dôme). Click here to see the photo gallery of the castle. The official trailer is availabe now:

Sponsors

SoCraTes-FR has three sponsors: Arolla, Novencia and Zenika. Their support helps us to make SoCraTes happens in France this year.

Agenda

The conference starts 24/09 at 17h00 and ends 26/09 at 17h00. The rooms at the castle are available at 16h00 on Thursday.

How to get to the “Château de Rochegude” ?

The “château de Rochegude” is located 1h20’ from Marseille, 1h45’ from Lyon, 1h15’ from Montpellier and 1h from Aix-En-Provence.

By Train

The closest stations are :
  • “Gare d’Orange” - 12 km
  • “Gare TGV d’Avignon” - 40 km

By Plane

  • Avignon Airport - 50 km
  • Marseille Airport - 120 km

SoCraTes Family

SoCraTes-FR is a part of a big family. The first “Software Craftsmanship and Testing (un)conference” took place a few years ago in Germany and is organised every year since 2011.

Other conferences have emerged in other European countries such as SoCraTes UK and SoCraTes Canaries.
